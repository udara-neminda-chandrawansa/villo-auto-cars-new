Our History

  • 01

    Founded in 2010

    Villo Auto Cars opened its doors in Nugegoda with a mission to deliver quality vehicles at fair prices. Our early days were built on passion and dedication.

  • 02

    Expanding Our Services

    We expanded into offering full vehicle documentation, after-sales services, and leasing assistance, making the buying experience seamless.

  • 03

    New Showroom Opening

    Our spacious new showroom opened, allowing us to display a wide range of vehicles and welcome more customers in comfort and style.

  • 04

    Going Digital

    We launched our website and social platforms, enabling customers across Sri Lanka to view our inventory and connect with us online with ease.
